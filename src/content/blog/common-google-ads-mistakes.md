Google Ads can be your golden ticket to getting your business seen by the right people right when they’re searching for what you offer. But let’s be honest – it’s not always smooth sailing. Some pretty common mistakes could be costing you clicks, conversions, and cold, hard cash.

We’re here to pull back the curtain on these blunders and give you the know-how to avoid them. Ready to level up your Google Ads game? Let’s dive in!

1. **The Jumbled Mess: Poor Account Structure**

Think of your Google Ads account like a filing cabinet—the foundation for organising your efforts to grow your business. If everything’s just tossed in randomly, good luck finding anything, let alone scaling your success! The same goes for your campaigns and ad groups. A jumbled mess makes management tough and wastes the budget that could fuel your growth.

The Fix: Get organised! Group campaigns by themes, use clear and consistent naming conventions and tailor ad groups to specific keywords and landing pages. 2. **The Unwanted Guests: Ignoring Negative Keywords**

Negative keywords are like your Google Ads bouncer, keeping the wrong kind of traffic from crashing your party. When you create a Google Ad campaign, it’s crucial to include negative keywords from the get-go to prevent your ads from appearing for searches that have nothing to do with your business. Without them, you risk wasted clicks and a drained budget.

The Fix: Don’t just set and forget your keywords. Review your search terms report to spot irrelevant terms and add them to your negative keyword list. This keeps your ads focused on the searches that matter.

3. **The Mobile Mishap: Neglecting Mobile Optimisation**

Raise your hand if you’ve ever searched for something on your phone! It’s pretty much everyone these days. And when it comes to advertising your business on Google, it’s important to remember that a considerable chunk of those searches happen on mobile devices. So, if your Google Ads aren’t optimised for mobile, you’re missing out on a massive opportunity to reach potential customers right when they’re looking for what you offer.

The Fix: Make sure your ads and landing pages are responsive, meaning they look great and function seamlessly on all screen sizes. Use mobile-specific features like click-to-call buttons or location extensions to make it easy for people to contact you or find your business. Also, consider creating mobile-specific landing pages that are streamlined for smaller screens. These tweaks will keep those mobile clicks (and conversions) coming!

4. **The Missed Opportunity: Overlooking Ad Extensions**

Ad extensions are like the icing on the cake – they add extra info to your ads and make them more enticing to click, potentially lowering your paid search advertising cost per click. Whether it’s site links, callouts, or structured snippets, there’s an extension to suit every goal, giving you more bang for your buck.

The Fix: Don’t skip the extensions! Add relevant ones to your ads to boost their visibility and give searchers more reasons to choose your business.

5. **The Blindfold: Not Tracking Conversions**

Tracking conversions is like having a GPS for your Google Ads campaigns – it tells you if you’re headed in the right direction. Without it, you’re flying blind and won’t know which ads or keywords are actually driving sales or leads.
