So, you just got a website created for your business in Northern beaches and pondering whether to go for SEO (Search Engine Optimisation) or not?

Experts and analysts from all over the world believe that SEO is alive and kicking! However, they do believe that the approach towards website optimisation needs to be changed drastically to keep up with the ever-changing search engines.

Let’s take a look at some points that justify your decision to go for organic SEO investment for your online business:

- **SEO Is Still In The Game!**

Don’t forget that search engines, including Google still consider the On-page and off-page [Search Engine Optimisation](/digital-marketing/seo/) done on the websites. That’s the reason why even today, the techniques of boosting SEO are still so much in demand. This statement is backed by numerous studies that are done by experts after different updates from search engines.

- **SEO Is Here To Stay!**

SEO isn’t going to lose its value and effectiveness anytime soon. By simply keeping a track of how search engines are developing in terms of algorithmic domination, you can figure out that how true it is.

- **It Is Affordable – Even For Small Businesses!**

Don’t believe the myth that SEO is costly. Someone unaware with Search Engine Optimisation might find it costly initially, but not in the long run. SEO has immense power to provide a remarkable amount of returns, unlike other digital marketing methods like [PPC advertising](/digital-marketing/google-ads/) and [social media promotion](/digital-marketing/social-media/). Social media builds your brand’s image, and PPC might deliver more returns, still Search Engine Optimisation is a multi-dimensional core of your online presence.

- **You Can’t Ignore The Importance Of Mobile And Local SEO**

The current pattern in the realm of SEO is for the mobile and local search optimisation. Experts believe that in near future the amount of traffic to various mobile devices shall exceed that of desktops. The developing significance of local and mobile traffic is another indicator that new productive methods should focus on mobile responsive websites. As mobile turns into a hot area for SEO, it’s quite clear why Search Engine Optimisation can’t be ignored and how important it has become to hire an SEO expert.

- **Search Engines Have Become An Excellent Source For Info And Things**

It has been found in various studies that before making a purchase, more that 85% of users are using different search engines to look for online reviews about the product or services. Isn’t this percentage enough to say that how handy and reliable the search engines have become! Currently, there is no other competitor that can overcome search engines, so in the future; this percentage is likely to increase.
