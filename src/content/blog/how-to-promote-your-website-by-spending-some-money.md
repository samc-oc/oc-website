There is a need to spend money on advertising online because in today’s world the market is filled with thousand of websites providing services and products similar to your website. This is why paid promotional techniques have surely become an integral part to make any marketing strategy a success. Following are few areas and types of advertising where you should invest to promote your website:

- **Adwords:**

Google makes billions of dollars consistently from the combo of Adwords-Adsense. It is one the most effective way to produce traffic for your site. It is based on a bidding system and your ads get published with search results whenever a user searches for something. - **Site-Specific Adwords:**

When you consider the Adsense units you would find that the majority of these have a link “Advertise on this site”. This helps Adwords advertisers to generate customized ads which would be published only on few specific websites. These work on the basis of CPM (cost per 1000 impressions) instead of a CPC (cost for every click). These can create a highly targeted traffic, this happens because you would be in full control of where the ad should be displayed.

- **Text Link Ads:**

You can buy some text link ads, if you’re looking forward to enhance search engine rankings of the website. This would ultimately help to leverage organic traffic. These text links not only generate traffic, but also improve the search rankings, especially if you place the links on relevant and authoritative sites. There are a few places where you can purchase text links.

- **Direct Banner Sales:**

Some of the renowned websites often sell banner ads or sponsorship ads. These are structured on weekly or monthly fees without considering the number of clicks or impressions your sites get. Banner placement can deliver value for money as create visibility and brand awareness, also drives in traffic to the website. The users who see this banner will become curious and may visit the site to check what the buzz is all about.

- **Blog Networks:**

There are few bog networks that are based on traffic exchange principle. You are supposed to create an account for blog and then earn credits by visiting blog’s of other members. Later, these credits are used to make others visit your blog, resulting in increased traffic.
