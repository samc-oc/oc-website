People are often told that layout is important for any website which is true. But what is equally important is some quality content that attracts people. Google has always focused on quality content, so you cannot really ignore the need of authentic and quality content. This should be done to rank well on search engines and to convert visitors into customers. You may have an idea about website content, but still consider these points:

- Always prefer to write attention seeking headlines and a captivating copy.

- You may have hardly visited any site that has a ‘Welcome to’ sign posted on the home page, so try to avoid such lines. You hardly get any time to grab the attention of the visitor, so do not waste time in using unimportant lines. Instead try to provide a relevant and well written copy on its pages.

- After you succeed in grabbing the attention of the site visitors, your next job is to keep them engaged in reading your page content/copy.
