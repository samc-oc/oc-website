Your website should be the pioneer for attracting new business and leads for your business. However, if your website doesn’t meet your business goals and bring in the numbers you want, it’s time to look at what you could do better to improve your success. The most effective way to convert traffic to a business is with a high-converting landing page. Here are some of our tips for developing one.

### Use a captivating header that draws people in

It’s a common one that you’ve no doubt seen everywhere, but it’s true! An unappealing title can turn heads in an instant as it’s the first thing people are likely to see when they load your page. Therefore, you want your headline to suggest benefits for the user while making it direct and confident. That way, your audience will know exactly what to expect and will continue through with your call to action button. However, you may need more than a title alone for that to happen.

Appearances can make or break a sale, making it hard to [design a website landing page](/web-development/). If your company emits a unique personality, then utilising all these things can help it shine and stand out from the competition. ### Make it work on smartphones!

Right now, the world without the internet is unimaginable. Soon, this will be the same with smartphones. According to [Statista](https://www.statista.com/statistics/330695/number-of-smartphone-users-worldwide/), over 6 billion people use smartphones. This number is expected to rise above 7.5 billion in the next five years. The point we’re trying to make is that using a smartphone to access the internet is not a niche, and many people rely on their smartphones for everyday internet use. It’s more than likely that a large percentage of visitors to your site will be through a smartphone, so make sure to design a landing page that translates well to all mobile layouts, portrait and landscape. An unreadable and messy website will stand no chance of success.
