When selecting the finest IT firm for your Australian business, there are numerous factors to consider. Some specialize in areas that aren't a good fit for your needs. When looking for an IT Company to look after you, here are some things to keep in mind.

### 1. What services can they provide?

The company you end up working with should be able to offer a comprehensive list of services that covers all the bases. These would include but not be limited to IT Support and Managed Services, Website design and development, eCommerce solutions, Digital Marketing, Copywriting, SEO Services, and more. Knowing that all your IT is handled by one provider, rather than having numerous firms and not knowing who to call when you need support, will save you a lot of time. We can be your one-stop [IT support service provider](/managed-it-services/) for all these services.

### 2. Do they have experience with businesses in your industry?

The company you pick should have a good understanding of businesses in your particular industry or at least be able to learn about your industry quickly. This knowledge will come in handy when developing strategies tailored to your specific needs, like [IT disaster recovery and backup](/blog/heres-why-you-need-an-it-disaster-recovery-plan-for-your-business/) plans.

### 3. What is their track record like?

When looking at potential IT firms, check out their track record. The last thing you want is to partner with a company with a history of poor performance. Testimonials are an excellent approach to learning more about a business, and you may even inquire with the IT firm about their favourite customers. They would be delighted to connect you with people who could tell you all there is to know about how they operate.

### 4. Are they up to date with the latest technology?

One of the biggest questions to ask an IT Company is how up to date they are with the latest tech and trends in the industry. The IT landscape is constantly changing and evolving. It would be best to make sure that the company you're working with keeps up with the latest trends and developments. Otherwise, you'll quickly fall behind the competition.

### 5. Do they have a good support system in place?

No matter how good a company is, things can always go wrong. That's why it's important to ensure that they have a good support system in place. Ask how they handle emergencies as a managed IT service provider, and you'll find out how you will be protected from any disaster. This way, you can rest assured that help is just a phone call or email away.
