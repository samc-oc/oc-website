In the digital age, advertising can make or break your business success. Google Ads, formerly Google AdWords, is a potent online advertising platform offering unparalleled opportunities to reach your target audience and boost conversions. [Google PPC (Pay-Per-Click) is immensely important](/blog/google-ppc-is-more-important-than-ever-for-your-business/) in today's digital marketing landscape. It provides businesses with a highly effective and targeted approach to reach potential customers online. By displaying ads to users actively searching for products or services related to their business, Google PPC ensures maximum relevancy, significantly increasing the chances of conversions.

However, managing [Google ads, especially for small businesses](/blog/google-adwords-tips-for-small-business-owners/), can be complex, and if not done correctly, it can lead to wasted resources and missed opportunities. In this article, we'll explore ten signs that indicate your business needs an ads strategist to optimise your advertising efforts and improve your conversion rate.

1. **Stagnant or Declining Performance**

If you've noticed stagnant or declining performance in your Google Ads campaigns, it clearly indicates that something needs to change. A Google ads specialist can thoroughly audit your existing campaigns, identify weaknesses, and devise a targeted strategy to turn things around. They can optimise your ads, keywords, and targeting by making data-driven adjustments to drive better results.

2. **Low Click-through Rates (CTR)**

A low CTR is a telltale sign that your ads aren't effectively engaging your audience. An experienced Google Ads consultant can create compelling ad copies that resonate with your potential customers, enticing them to click through to your website. Higher CTR improves your Quality Score and drives more relevant traffic to your site.

3. **High Cost Per Click (CPC)**

If your Google Ads budget is drained due to high CPC rates without a corresponding increase in conversions, it's time to seek professional assistance. An ads specialist can optimise your bidding strategy, select the right keywords, and improve your ad relevance, resulting in lower CPC and better ROI.

4. **Inadequate Keyword Research**

Effective keyword research is the cornerstone of successful Google Ads campaigns. If your ads do not target the right keywords, you may miss out on valuable traffic. An ads expert will conduct comprehensive keyword research to identify high-converting, relevant keywords that align with your business objectives.

5. **Ignoring Mobile Users**

With most internet users now accessing the web via mobile devices, ignoring mobile optimisation can harm your ad performance. A skilled Google Ads consultant understands the importance of mobile-friendly ads and landing pages, ensuring seamless experiences for mobile users and improving conversion rates.

6. **Lack of Tracking and Analytics**

To measure the effectiveness of your Google Ads campaigns, you need accurate tracking and analytics in place. If you're unsure about how to set up and interpret these metrics, an ads expert can help you integrate tracking tools, analyse the data, and provide actionable insights to refine your strategies continually.

7. **Overlooking Ad Extensions**

Ad extensions offer additional information and enticing calls to action that can significantly boost the performance of your ads. If you're not using ad extensions or not using them effectively, you're missing an opportunity to stand out from competitors and provide more value to your audience.

8. **Limited Time and Resources**

Running successful Google Ads campaigns requires time, effort, and expertise. If your internal team lacks the necessary experience or is stretched thin with other responsibilities, partnering with an ads expert allows you to focus on core business operations while knowing your advertising efforts are in capable hands.

9. **Falling Behind Competitors**

Are your competitors outperforming you in the online advertising arena? A Google Ads strategist can analyse your competitors' strategies, identify their strengths and weaknesses, and develop a competitive plan that positions your business for success.

10. **Underwhelming Conversion Rate**

Ultimately, the success of your Google Ads campaign hinges on the conversion rate. If your ads are driving traffic but not converting visitors into customers, a Google Ads expert can implement A/B testing, landing page optimisation, and other conversion rate optimisation (CRO) techniques to maximise your ROI.

Google Ads can be a game-changer for your business, but to harness its full potential, you may need the expertise of a Google Ads consultant. From [Google Ads PPC management in Sydney](/digital-marketing/google-ads/) to utilising the best way to advertise your business, a skilled expert can revitalise your campaigns, improve your conversion rate, and propel your business to new heights in the competitive digital landscape.
